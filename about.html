About SSV Homes

Built on trust, quality, and long-term value creation.

Professionally Driven Excellence

SSV Homes is a forward-thinking real estate company built on trust, quality, and long-term value creation. Founded in 2010, SSV Homes has established itself as a professionally driven organization with a strong presence across residential development, colonisation, investment, and real estate marketing.

With a refined approach to planning and execution, we focus on delivering developments that meet modern lifestyle expectations while ensuring sustainable growth for our clients and partners.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as a trusted and progressive real estate brand that sets benchmarks in design, execution, and customer experience. We aim to build well-planned communities that offer comfort, connectivity, and long-term appreciation, while continuously evolving with market trends and innovation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create premium living spaces and high-potential developments that stand the test of time. We are committed to delivering excellence through superior construction standards, transparent processes, and strategic project planning. Alongside development, we provide end-to-end marketing solutions that enhance project visibility and maximize returns, ensuring value for homeowners, investors, and stakeholders alike.

Founder’s Story

SSV Homes was founded by Mr. Piyush Sharma, a visionary entrepreneur with deep insight into the real estate ecosystem. Driven by a desire to raise industry standards, he established SSV Homes to deliver developments that balance design, functionality, and investment value.

His hands-on leadership and customer-centric philosophy have played a key role in shaping the company’s steady growth and strong market reputation. Under his guidance, SSV Homes continues to redefine premium living through innovation and integrity.
