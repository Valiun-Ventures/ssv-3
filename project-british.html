Project Overview

British Grand is SSVH's prestigious completed project featuring luxury villas with authentic British colonial architecture. Spread across 20 acres of prime land in Indore, this exclusive community offers a perfect blend of old-world charm and modern amenities.

Each villa is meticulously designed with spacious interiors, private gardens, and premium finishes. The project features villa sizes ranging from 1800 to 3600 sq.ft, making it ideal for families seeking luxury living with a touch of heritage.
