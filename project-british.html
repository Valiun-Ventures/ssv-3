Contact Us Download Brochure

Project Overview

Singapore British Grand is a large-scale, premium plotted residential development designed to offer a superior lifestyle with easy access to Indore city. Developed by Sarthak Real Build Pvt. Ltd., this multi-block township spans across a significant area, featuring multiple residential sectors and wide internal roads.

The township is meticulously planned with approximately 340 to 350 residential plots. Each sector is connected by well-designed infrastructure, ensuring direct connectivity to AB Road, Manglia, and Dhabili.
