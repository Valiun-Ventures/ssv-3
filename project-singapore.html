Project Overview

Singapore Radhakunj by Sarthak Real Build Pvt. Ltd. is a premium residential plotted township located near Singapore Township Phase-1, Indore. Spanning approximately 8 – 10 Acres, this project offers a perfect blend of residential comfort and commercial potential.

The township features 120+ plots including residential, HIG, and shop-cum-commercial configurations. Strategically positioned near A.B. Road, it provides excellent connectivity to Manglia Industrial Area and leading townships.
